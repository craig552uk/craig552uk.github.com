Course Data Web Scraper

The University of Leicester wanted to publish information about their postgraduate courses in a formatted XML feed.

As this information was already published on their web site, I wrote a scraper to extract course information from it and republish it in the required XML format.

Twitter Impact Data Analysis

Following the the discovery of Richard III, the University of Leicester wanted to measure to global impact of interest of this discovery on social media.

I created a program which downloaded all tweets mentioning the discovery and analysed the social reach of those tweeters. This allowed the university to identify influential individuals in their social sphere.
